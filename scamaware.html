Scam aware link

I have created ScamAware to make Facebook a safer place. Users can report which Facebook accounts have scammed them personally. A reported account gets added to a database of confirmed scam accounts once a report is filed. This way, others can search whether the Facebook user has been reported previously before trusting or dealing with them.


My goal with ScamAware is to give people an easy way to share their experience and warn others. By reporting scammers they've encountered, users build a resource that exposes the dishonest accounts. The site's purpose is to raise awareness and make it easier for everyone to avoid getting scammed on social media.
